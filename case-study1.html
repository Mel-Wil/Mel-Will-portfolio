BRIEF

Eco lets any home or apartment seeker find
their place online. Payments are easy and
fast for all of our people.

CLIENT

Eco realtor corp

ROLE

UX/ UI designer and Usability Analyst

LOCATION

Remote

Challenge.

Users can not easily find the apartments or houses that they want
in the right time and that meets all the requirements they need.
The payments were not easily sent from tenant to the landlord,
which caused conflict on the app. Due to this, the users keep
leaving the app, hence causing the app to loose much money
each month.

Goal.

Making the app more user friendly. One that will allow users
to find the right place in the right time. And the payment be
made on the app instantly, and ensuring that the landlords
receive their money. The app will also be interactive, that
the users will not have problem navigating through the app.

Approach.

The resulting wireframe from the design thinking
and weeks of working on the design.

app with tons of features like dynamic landing page,
